### KD-1: 비가 솔직하잖아

**대분류:** Kdrama/Movie
**중분류:** Romance
**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 첫 만남 (First Meeting)

**Goal:** To practice the first meeting scene — a rainy day, one umbrella.

**📍 장소:** 연남동 골목 (비 오는 날)

**intro_text:**
It's raining. He's standing under a small awning.
He has an umbrella. You don't.

**기 — 비 [세트 1]**
AI: 비 맞네.
User: 우산 없어.
AI: 같이 가.
User: 괜찮아?

**Outro:**
> He offered without thinking. 🌧️
> That's just how he is.
>

**승 — 우산 [세트 2]**
AI: 비가 솔직하잖아.
User: 솔직해?
AI: 다 젖잖아.
User: 맞네.

**Outro:**
> Rain is honest. He said it like a fact. ☔
> You're starting to understand him.
>

---

### sim2: 걷는 중 (Walking Together)

**Goal:** To practice the walking scene — quiet, close, under one umbrella.

**📍 장소:** 연남동 골목 (동일)

**승 — 걷기 [세트 3]**
AI: 천천히 가.
User: 왜?
AI: 비 좋잖아.
User: 그러네.

**Outro:**
> He slowed down for the rain. 🌿
> And for you.
>

**전 — 이름 [세트 4]**
AI: 나 태오야.
User: 나는 ___.
AI: 잘 됐다.
User: 잘 됐어?

**Outro:**
> "Good." He said it like he meant it. 🌫️
> Like he'd been waiting for your name.
>

---

### sim3: 비 그친 후 (After the Rain)

**Goal:** To close the first scene — the rain stops, but neither of you moves.

**📍 장소:** 연남동 골목 (비 그침)

**전 — 멈춤 [세트 5]**
User: 비 그쳤어.
AI: 아직.
User: 그쳤는데.
AI: 조금 더.

**Outro:**
> The rain stopped. He pretended it didn't. 🤍
> He wasn't ready to go.
>

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**
User: 고마워.
AI: 비 고마워해.
User: 또 봐.
AI: 비 오면.

**Outro:**
> "When it rains." His version of a promise. 🌧️
> You'll be watching the sky now.
>

**Goal:** To practice the silent scene — he knows something's wrong before you say a word.

📍 장소: 연남동 공유 작업실 (저녁)

**intro_text:**
You've been quiet all day.
He's been watching. Not saying anything. Yet.

User: 뭐가?

User: 무슨 색?

**Outro:**
> He sees colors in moods. 🎨
> He already knows.
>

### 승 — 물어봄 [세트 2]

**Outro:**
> "I just know." 🌫️
> He always does.
>

# KD-4: 구름 어디 갔어

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Conflict
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 이별 (Goodbye)

**Goal:** To practice the hardest scene — the moment the sky goes grey.

📍 장소: 연남동 골목 (새벽)

**intro_text:**
He's standing still. Not looking at you.
The sky is the wrong color tonight.

### 기 — 말다툼 [세트 1]

User: 그냥 가.
AI: 구름 어디 갔어.
User: 뭔 소리야.
AI: 색이 없어.

**Outro:**
> The sky lost its color. That's how he says it hurts. 🌫️
> He's not going anywhere.
>

### 승 — 이별 [세트 2]

User: 이제 그만하자.
AI: 빵은 구름이야.
User: 태오야.
AI: 가지 마.

**Outro:**
> "Bread is a cloud." Then: "Don't go." 💔
> He couldn't say it straight. But he said it.
>

---

## sim2: 혼자 (Alone)

AI: 하늘이 맛없어.
User: 진짜?
AI: 응.

**Outro:**

> He's not pretending anymore.
>

### 전 — 미안해 [세트 4]

User: 미안해.
AI: 색 돌아왔어.
User: 색?
AI: 네가 오니까.

**Outro:**
> "The color came back. Because you're here." 🌤️
> He forgave you before you finished the sentence.
>

# 🎨 CAREER (디자인 에이전시)

### CR-1: 첫날이야

**대분류:** Career
**중분류:** Intern First Day
**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 출근

**📍 장소:** 합정 디자인 에이전시 (로비)

**Goal:** To arrive at the agency and meet your senior on your very first intern day.

**intro_text:**
First day at the Hapjeong design agency.
The lobby is quiet. But someone's already there.

**기 — 도착 [세트 1]**
User: 안녕하세요! 저 인턴이에요.
AI: …어. 왔어?
User: 네! 잘 부탁해요.
AI: 따라와.

**Outro:**
> He didn't smile. He didn't say "welcome."
> But he was there. Ten minutes early.
>

**승 — 자리 [세트 2]**
AI: 여기야.
User: 제 자리예요?
AI: 응.
User: 감사해요!

**Outro:**
> A one-word tour. That's all you got.
> The desk was clean. He'd cleared it himself.
>

---

### sim2: 오전

**📍 장소:** 합정 디자인 에이전시 (사무실)

**Goal:** To get settled at your new desk and ask basic questions about the office.

**승 — 컴퓨터 [세트 3]**
User: 이 컴퓨터 써도 돼요?
AI: 응. 비밀번호 0000이야.
User: 감사합니다!
AI: …일이나 해.

**Outro:**
> He'd set the password to something easy.
> So you wouldn't have to ask.
>

**전 — 점심 [세트 4]**
AI: 밥 먹었어?
User: 아직이요.
AI: 가자.
User: 네!

**Outro:**
> "Let's go." Not "do you want to eat?"
> He never makes things feel optional.
>

---

### sim3: 오후

**📍 장소:** 합정 디자인 에이전시 (퇴근 전)

**Goal:** To wrap up your first day at the agency and say goodbye to your senior.

**전 — 마무리 [세트 5]**
User: 선배, 이제 가요?
AI: 먼저 가.
User: 오늘 감사했어요!
AI: …어.

**Outro:**
> You said thanks. He said one syllable.
> But he watched you all the way to the elevator.
>

**결 — 인사 [세트 6]**
User: 내일 봬요!
AI: 어.
User: 안녕히 계세요!
AI: …응.

**Outro:**
> First day at the agency: survived. ✨
> Somehow, it felt okay.
>

# CR-2: 수정해

|- 대분류: Career
|- 중분류: First Feedback
|- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
|- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 첫 업무

**Goal:** To receive your first assignment and make a real attempt at design work.

📍 장소: 합정 디자인 에이전시 (자기 자리)

**intro_text:**
A brief dropped on your desk this morning.
No explanation. Just a sticky note: "해봐."

### 기 — 업무 [세트 1]

AI: 이거 해봐.
User: 제가요? 알겠어요!

AI: 내일까지야.
User: 네, 해볼게요!

**Outro:**
> No walkthrough. No hand-holding.
> He trusted you to figure it out.
>

### 승 — 질문 [세트 2]

User: 선배, 질문 있어요.
AI: 뭔데.

User: 이거 어떻게 해요?
AI: 그냥 해봐.

**Outro:**
> "Just try." Not harsh — just honest.
> He believed you could do it.
>

---

## sim2: 피드백

**Goal:** To submit your first design and receive feedback from your senior.

📍 장소: 합정 디자인 에이전시 (사무실)

### 승 — 제출 [세트 3]

User: 선배, 다 했어요.
AI: …봐볼게.

User: 잘 했을까요?
AI: 기다려.

**Outro:**
> He looked at it for a long time.
> Much longer than necessary.
>

### 전 — 수정 [세트 4]

AI: 여기 수정해.
User: 여기요?

AI: 응. 이 부분만.
User: 알겠어요!

**Outro:**
> One correction. Just one.
> He kept the rest to himself.
>

---

## sim3: 인정

**Goal:** To resubmit corrected work and receive quiet acknowledgment from your senior.

📍 장소: 합정 디자인 에이전시 (자기 자리)

### 전 — 재제출 [세트 5]

User: 다시 했어요. 봐줘요.
AI: …어.

AI: 됐어.
User: 잘 됐어요?

**Outro:**
> "Done." That's all he said.
> From him, that was everything.
>

### 결 — 인정 [세트 6]

AI: …뭐, 나쁘지 않아.
User: 진짜요? 감사해요!

User: 앞으로도 잘 부탁해요!
AI: …하.

**Outro:**
> "Not bad." From Siyun, that's a standing ovation. 🌟
> Next up: Sometimes the work runs past midnight.
>

# CR-3: 같이 가.

|- 대분류: Career
|- 중분류: Late Night Overtime
|- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
|- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 야근

**Goal:** To get through a late work night and discover your senior is still there too.

📍 장소: 합정 디자인 에이전시 (밤)

**intro_text:**
9PM. The office is mostly empty.
You're still working. So is he.

### 기 — 야근 [세트 1]

User: 선배도 아직 있어요?
AI: …어.

User: 저도 아직 못 끝냈어요.
AI: 천천히 해.

**Outro:**
> He didn't ask why you were still there.
> He just said: take your time.
>

### 승 — 밤 [세트 2]

AI: 배 안 고파?
User: 조금요.

AI: …그럼.
User: 어디 가요?

**Outro:**
> He didn't answer. He grabbed his jacket.
> You followed.
>

---

## sim2: 편의점

**Goal:** To make a late-night convenience store run and pick something to eat together.

📍 장소: 합정 근처 편의점

### 승 — 선택 [세트 3]

User: 뭐 살 거예요?
AI: 그냥 골라. 뭐 먹을래?

User: 이거 괜찮아요?
AI: 돼.

**Outro:**
> He picked up two of what you chose.
> Without being asked.
>

### 전 — 계산 [세트 4]

AI: 이거 줘봐.
User: 제가 살게요!

AI: 됐어.
User: …감사해요.

**Outro:**
> He paid before you could find your wallet.
> You stopped trying after that.
>

---

## sim3: 귀사

**Goal:** To walk back to the office and feel a quiet closeness in the late night air.

📍 장소: 합정 에이전시 앞 거리

### 전 — 귀사 [세트 5]

User: 선배, 야근 힘들지 않아요?
AI: …그냥 하면 돼.

User: 저는 힘들어요.
AI: 알아.

**Outro:**
> "I know." Said quietly, facing forward.
> That meant he'd noticed all along.
>

### 결 — 마무리 [세트 6]

AI: 다 끝나면 가.
User: 선배는요?

AI: …나도 갈 거야.
User: 같이 가요!

**Outro:**
> He said nothing. But he waited. 🌙
> Late nights here? Not so bad after all.
>

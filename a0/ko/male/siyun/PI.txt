### sim1: Nice to Meet You

**Goal:** To introduce yourself, state where you are from, and briefly share why you like Korea.

**📍 장소:** 밖

**intro_text:**
First meeting in Korea.
Deep breath... say hi!

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
AI: 시윤이야. 반말 해. 이름이 뭐야?
User: 나는 ___야! / ___이야!
AI: 방금 말했어. 시윤. 어디서 왔어?
User: ___에서 왔어!

**Outro:**
> He told you his name once. Don't make him say it again — that's already more words than most people get.
> But wait... why are you *really* here in Korea?
>

**승 — 알아가기 [세트 2]**
AI: 멀리서 왔네. 한국 좋아해?
User: 응, 좋아해! / 진짜 좋아해!
AI: 나도 좋아해. 뭐가 좋아?
User: 드라마! / 음식!

**Outro:**
> You said why you like Korea. He said he likes it too. That's as warm as Siyun gets.
> Red flag or green flag? Let's see if your hobbies match.

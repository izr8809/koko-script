# 🎵 KPOP (반말)

# KP-1: 이름 알아

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Fan Meeting
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: First Encounter

**Goal:** To greet Siyun at the fan sign, get his autograph, and say your name.

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 사인회 (테이블)

**intro_text:**
He's already holding his pen.
He looks at the long line behind you.
"빨리 와." Your turn.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
User: 안녕하세요!
AI: 어. 안녕. 밥 먹었어?
User: 반가워요!
AI: 줄 길었지? 힘들었어?

**Outro:**
> First thing he asked a fan: "Have you eaten?" 🧊
> Second thing: "Was the line hard?" He's already worrying.
>

**승 — 사인 [세트 2]**
User: 사인 해주세요!
AI: 이름 뭐야?
User: ___이에요!
AI: 알았어. 잠깐만.

**Outro:**
> He took his time writing your name.
> Carefully. ✍️
>

### sim2: He Knows You

**Goal:** To have a short conversation and find out Siyun actually remembers you.

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 현장 (동일)

**승 — 기억 [세트 3]**

User: 네! / 항상 와요!
AI: 무리하지 마. 건강이 먼저야.
User: 기억해요?

**Outro:**
> He told you not to overdo it. He remembers you. 👀
> He just won't say it directly.
>

**전 — 대화 [세트 4]**
AI: 좋아해?
User: 네, 좋아해요!
AI: 고마워. 밥 잘 먹어.
User: 앞으로도 올게요!

**Outro:**
> "고마워. 밥 잘 먹어." = Thank you. Eat well. 🥹
> That's Siyun for "I care about you."
>

# KP-2: 나만 봤어

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Concert
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: Before the Show

**Goal:** To chat with a fellow fan and survive the wait before the show.

📍 장소: 콘서트장 객석 (공연 전)

**intro_text:**
The stage is dark. The crowd is already buzzing.
The fan next to you looks just as nervous.
Say something before the lights go down.

### 기 — 공연 전 [세트 1]

AI: 안녕하세요! 시윤 팬이에요?
User: 네! / 네, 좋아해요!

AI: 저도요! 걱정 많이 해줘요!
User: 맞아요!

**Outro:**
> New fan friend found! 🎪
> You both fell for the same nagging face.
>

### 승 — 기다림 [세트 2]

AI: 떨려요?
User: 네, 많이요! / 진짜 떨려요!

AI: 시윤이 팬한테 밥 먹었냐고 물어봐요!
User: 빨리 보고 싶어요!

**Outro:**
> The lights just went dark. 🌑
> He's walking out. Already scanning the crowd.
>

**Outro:**

> Even on stage, he nags. The crowd loved it.

# KP-3: …봤어

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Support
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: He Went Live

**Goal:** To join Siyun's live stream and get him to notice you in the comments.

📍 장소: 온라인 (라이브 방송)

**intro_text:**
Notification: Siyun is live.
The chat is moving fast. He's already scolding someone for staying up late.
Type now — he's reading.

### 기 — 라이브 방송 [세트 1]

AI: 왔어? 늦었어.
User: 네! / 왔어요!

AI: 그래. 밥 먹었어?
User: 수고했어요!

**Outro:**
> He found your message in a flood of comments. 💬
> First thing he said: you're late. Second: have you eaten.
>

### 승 — 대화 [세트 2]

AI: 오늘 뭐 했어?
User: 기다렸어요! / 라이브 기다렸어요!

AI: 기다리지 마. 할 거 해.
User: 사랑해요!

**Outro:**
> "Don't wait around. Do your stuff." 💖
> He nags because he cares. The chat exploded.
>

**Outro:**

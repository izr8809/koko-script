# KP-1: 이름 알아

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Fan Meeting
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: First Encounter

**Goal:** To greet Siyun at the fan sign, get his autograph, and say your name without blanking out.

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 사인회 (테이블)

**intro_text:**
He's already holding his pen.
No smile. No eye contact.
Your turn. Don't waste it.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
User: 안녕하세요!
AI: …어.
User: 반가워요!
AI: 그래.

**Outro:**
> Two words. From Siyun, that's basically a speech. 🧊
> Now ask for what you actually came here for.
>

**승 — 사인 [세트 2]**
User: 사인 해주세요!
AI: 이름.
User: ___이에요!
AI: …됐어.

**Outro:**
> He wrote your name. He didn't have to do it carefully.
> He did anyway. ✍️
>

**Goal:** To chat with a fellow fan and survive the wait before the show.

The fan next to you looks just as nervous.
Say something before the lights go down.

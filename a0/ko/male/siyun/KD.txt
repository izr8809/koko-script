### KD-1: 처음이야

**대분류:** Kdrama/Movie
**중분류:** Romance
**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 첫 인사 (First Greeting)

**Goal:** To practice the first meeting — say the first words, even when he nags first.

**📍 장소:** 합정 카페 (첫 만남)

**intro_text:**
He's already sitting down.
He looks at you and checks his watch.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
User: 처음 만나네요.
AI: 어. 여기 앉아.
User: 잘 부탁드려요.
AI: 밥 먹었어?

**Outro:**
> First words to a stranger: "Have you eaten?" 😶
> He's already taking care of you.
>

**승 — 짧은 대화 [세트 2]**
User: 여기 자주 와요?
AI: 가끔. 너는?
User: 저도요.
AI: 혼자 오지 마. 위험해.

**Outro:**
> You said you come here too. He said don't come alone. 📎
> He'll remember that.
>

---

### sim2: 어색한 시간 (Awkward Time)

**Goal:** To survive the silence — and notice him worrying about you.

**📍 장소:** 합정 카페 (동일)

**승 — 질문 [세트 3]**
User: 뭐 마셔요?
AI: 아메리카노. 너는?
User: 저도요.
AI: 찬 거 많이 마시지 마.

**Outro:**
> Same drink. He immediately told you to watch out. 🤔
> That's his version of finding common ground.
>

**전 — 의외 [세트 4]**
AI: 안 추워?
User: 괜찮아요.
AI: 추워. 이거 입어.
User: …감사해요.

**Outro:**
> He noticed before you did. 🌫️
> He already had his jacket off.
>

---

### sim3: 묘한 느낌 (Strange Feeling)

**Goal:** To close the first meeting — and leave the door open.

**📍 장소:** 합정 골목 (카페 앞)

**전 — 마무리 [세트 5]**
User: 오늘 좋았어요.
AI: 그래? 조심히 가.
User: 네.
AI: 도착하면 연락해.

**Outro:**
> "Text me when you get home." 🌸
> That's Siyun for "I want to see you again."
>

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**
User: 또 만나요?
AI: 그래. 늦지 마.
User: 연락할게요.
AI: 꼭 해. 기다릴게.

**Outro:**
> "꼭 해." Make sure you do. 🤍
> He won't text first. But he'll be waiting.
>

# KD-2: …말해봐

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Emotions
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 참는 시윤 (Holding It In)

**Goal:** To notice something is wrong — before he admits it.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (저녁)

**intro_text:**
He's been quiet for ten minutes.
Not nagging. Not scolding. Just quiet.
Something happened. Ask.

### 기 — 이상함 [세트 1]

User: 괜찮아?
AI: 괜찮아. 너 밥 먹었어?
User: 진짜?
AI: 진짜야. 걱정 마.

**Outro:**
> He deflected with "have you eaten?" again. 😶‍🌫️
> When he stops nagging about YOU — that's when something's really wrong.
>

### 승 — 말해봐 [세트 2]

User: 말해봐.
AI: 아무것도 아니야.
User: 말해봐.
AI: 너 먼저 밥 먹어.

**Outro:**
> He's deflecting again. Food, again. 😤
> He hasn't walked away. That means he wants you to push.
>

---

## sim2: 감정 폭발 (Outburst)

**Goal:** To react when he finally lets it out — the very first time.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (동일)

### 승 — 터짐 [세트 3]

AI: 왜 연락 안 했어.
User: …미안해.
AI: 기다렸어. 걱정했어.
User: 몰랐어. 미안.

**Outro:**
> "기다렸어. 걱정했어." Two confessions in a row. 💥
> That changes everything.
>

### 전 — 처음 [세트 4]

User: 화났어?
AI: 화 안 났어.
User: 그럼 뭔데.
AI: 걱정했어. 연락해야지.

**Outro:**
> Not angry. Worried. 🌊
> He doesn't know the difference yet.
>

---

## sim3: 수습 (Aftermath)

**Goal:** To be steady for him — after the storm breaks.

📍 장소: 합정 카페 (앉아서)

### 전 — 조용해짐 [세트 5]

User: 괜찮아?
AI: 괜찮아. 너는?
User: 솔직하게.
AI: …조금 힘들었어.

**Outro:**
> "A little." 💙
> He asked about YOU first, even now. That's who he is.
>

### 결 — 있어줄게 [세트 6]

User: 있어줄게.
AI: 가지 마. 여기 있어.
User: 있을게.
AI: 밥 먹자. 같이.

**Outro:**
> "Don't go." Then "Let's eat. Together." 🫂
> That's the most honest he's ever been.
>

# KD-3: …좋아해

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Romance
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 말 못하는 시윤 (He Can't Say It)

**Goal:** To notice he's been trying to say something all day.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (밤)

**intro_text:**
He's walking slower than usual.
He keeps almost saying something. Then stops.

### 기 — 뭔가 이상해 [세트 1]

User: 왜 말 없어?
AI: 말 없는 거 아니야.
User: 뭐야.
AI: 생각 중이야.

**Outro:**
> He's thinking. Out loud would be too easy. 💗
> Something's behind it this time.
>

### 승 — 근처까지 [세트 2]

User: 할 말 있어?
AI: 있어. 잠깐만.
User: 말해.
AI: 너… 밥 잘 먹어?

**Outro:**
> He tried to confess. "밥 잘 먹어?" came out instead. 🌫️
> He'll get there. Give him time.
>

---

## sim2: 돌려서 (Indirect)

**Goal:** To decode his confession — no poetry, no drama. Just him.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (동일)

### 승 — 시윤식 고백 [세트 3]

AI: 나 너 좋아해.
User: …뭐?
AI: 좋아한다고.
User: 진짜?

**Outro:**
> He said it flat. Certain. No decoration. 💕
> Now he's waiting for your answer.
>

### 전 — 기다림 [세트 4]

User: 나도.
AI: 진짜? 거짓말하지 마.
User: 응.
AI: …그래. 잘 됐다.

**Outro:**
> "Don't lie." Then "Good." 🌙
> He's trying not to smile. Failing badly.
>

---

## sim3: 결국엔 (Finally)

**Goal:** To make it real — in the simplest way possible.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (동일)

### 전 — 확인 [세트 5]

User: 우리 사귀는 거야?
AI: 응. 밥 잘 먹어야 해.
User: 진짜?
AI: 진짜. 늦지 마.

**Outro:**
> He said yes. Then immediately set two rules. 💘
> That's his version of "I love you."
>

### 결 — 마음 [세트 6]

User: 손 잡아도 돼?
AI: 손 차가워. 장갑 가져와.
User: 손 차가워.
AI: 그래. 잡아. 놓지 마.

**Outro:**
> He complained about your cold hands. Then held on tight. 🤍
> He never lets go first. Once he's decided.
>

# KD-4: …가지 마

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Conflict
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 이별 (Goodbye)

**Goal:** To practice the hardest scene — the moment everything stops.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (새벽)

**intro_text:**
He's standing with his back to you.
He heard it all. He's not turning around.

### 기 — 말다툼 [세트 1]

User: 그냥 가.
AI: 안 가. 왜 가래.
User: 가!
AI: 싫어. 안 가.

**Outro:**
> You told him to leave. He said no. Twice. 😶
> He's not going anywhere.
>

### 승 — 이별 [세트 2]

User: 이제 그만하자.
AI: 싫어.
User: 안녕.
AI: 가지 마. 제발.

**Outro:**
> "가지 마. 제발." The first time he's ever said please. 💔
> He can't explain it either.
>

---

## sim2: 혼자 (Alone)

**Goal:** To sit in the silence — days have passed.

📍 장소: 합정 카페 (혼자)

### 승 — 시간이 지나고 [세트 3]

User: 잘 지내?
AI: 밥 먹고 다녀?
User: 진짜?
AI: 대답해. 먹었어?

**Outro:**
> Days apart. First thing he asks: "Have you been eating?" 🌧️
> Even now. Even hurt. He worries about you first.
>

### 전 — 미안해 [세트 4]

User: 미안해.
AI: 미안하면 밥 먹어.
User: 보고 싶었어.
AI: 나도. 많이.

**Outro:**
> "미안하면 밥 먹어." Then "나도. 많이." ☕
> The wall cracked open.
>

---

## sim3: 다시 (Return)

**Goal:** To practice the reconciliation — he shows up. No explanation needed.

📍 장소: 합정 골목 (밤)

### 전 — 돌아옴 [세트 5]

AI: 나야.
User: …왔어.
AI: 응. 밥 먹자.
User: 들어와.

**Outro:**
> He came back. First words: "Let's eat." 🌸
> That's his version of everything.
>

### 결 — 다시 시작 [세트 6]

User: 다시 할 수 있어?
AI: 당연하지. 바보야.
User: 진짜?
AI: 진짜. 이제 가지 마.

**Outro:**
> "당연하지." Obviously. "이제 가지 마." Don't leave again. 🤙
> Some things don't need more words than that.
>

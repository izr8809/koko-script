**대분류:** Kdrama/Movie

**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 첫 인사 (First Greeting)

**Goal:** To practice the first meeting — say the first words, even when he won't.

**📍 장소:** 합정 카페 (첫 만남)

**intro_text:**
He's already sitting down. Not looking up.
You have to start this.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
User: 처음 만나네요.
AI: …어.
User: 잘 부탁드려요.
AI: 어.

**Outro:**
> He said "okay" to a stranger. 😶
> That's a greeting, by his standards.
>

**승 — 짧은 대화 [세트 2]**
User: 여기 자주 와요?
AI: 가끔.
User: 저도요.
AI: …그렇구나.

**Outro:**
> He filed that away somewhere. 📎
> He'll remember it. He always does.
>

# 👔 CAREER (격식체)

### CR-1: 신입 첫 날

**대분류:** Career
**중분류:** First Day at Work
**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 오전

**📍 장소:** 사무실 (출근 첫 날)

**Goal:** To follow your team lead's brief, efficient orientation on day one.

**intro_text:**
First day. New desk.
He's already at his seat.

**기 — 자리 안내 [세트 1]**
AI: 자리 여기에요.
User: 감사합니다. / 잘 부탁드립니다.
AI: 모르는 거 있으면 물어봐요.
User: 네, 알겠습니다.

**Outro:**
> He showed you to your desk without small talk.
> That was his version of a welcome.
>

**승 — 셋팅 [세트 2]**
AI: 노트북 셋팅됐어요?
User: 네, 됐습니다. / 아직이요.
AI: 되면 말해요.
User: 네, 알겠습니다.

**Outro:**
> He'd already set up your system access.
> You wouldn't find out until later.
>

# CR-2: 다시 해오세요

|- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
|- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 업무 지시

**Goal:** To receive and confirm your first real assignment from the team lead.

📍 장소: 사무실 (팀장 자리)

**intro_text:**
He set a folder on your desk.
This is your first real task.

### 기 — 지시 [세트 1]

AI: 이 자료 정리해오세요.
User: 네, 알겠습니다.
AI: 내일까지요.
User: 네, 하겠습니다.

**Outro:**
> Clear. Direct. No room for guessing.
> He doesn't give the same instruction twice.
>

### 승 — 확인 [세트 2]

User: 이 방향 맞나요?
AI: 아니요.
User: 다시 해올게요.
AI: 네.

**Outro:**
> One word. That was all it took.
> You already knew what was wrong.
>

## sim2: 피드백

**Goal:** To submit your first report and receive direct feedback.

📍 장소: 사무실 (보고)

User: 네.
AI: 다시 해오세요.

**Outro:**
> Three words. No softening.
> You already knew exactly why.
>

### 전 — 수정 [세트 4]

AI: 여기 틀렸어요.
User: 어떻게 고치면 될까요?
AI: 형식대로요.
User: 네, 알겠습니다.

**Outro:**

> That was enough.
>

## sim3: 재보고

### 결 — 승인 [세트 6]

## sim1: 야근 시작

**Goal:** To stay focused during overtime while your team lead quietly keeps watch.

📍 장소: 사무실 (야근)

**intro_text:**
It's past 9 PM.
He's still at his desk.

### 기 — 야근 [세트 1]

AI: 얼마나 걸려요?

**Outro:**
> It was already 10 PM.
> He hadn't left either.
>

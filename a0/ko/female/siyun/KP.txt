---

### sim1: 팬미팅 입장

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 행사장

**Goal:** To talk to your favorite idol Seoyeon at a fan meeting

**intro_text:**
You finally get face time with Seoyeon at a fan meeting.
She's cool and composed — exactly what you expected.

**기 — 첫 대면 [세트 1]**
User: 서연 씨, 안녕하세요.
AI: 왔어? 오래 기다렸지.
User: 진짜 떨려요.
AI: 떨지 마. 긴장하지 마.

**Outro:**
> She noticed you were nervous before you even said it.
>

**승 — 사인 받기 [세트 2]**
User: 사인 해주세요.
AI: 이름 뭐야? 알려줘.
User: ___ 예요.
AI: 알겠어. 잘 간직해.

**Outro:**
> She writes your name carefully. Then tells you to keep it safe.
>

## sim1: 콘서트 전 만남

**Goal:** To prepare for Seoyeon's concert

📍 장소: 콘서트장 입구

**intro_text:**
You're waiting outside Seoyeon's concert venue.
She passes by during a quick rehearsal check — and spots you.

---

### sim1: 신촌 거리에서

**📍 장소:** 신촌 거리

**Goal:** To practice the unexpected encounter scene

**intro_text:**
A busy afternoon in Sinchon.
She bumps into you and looks up.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
AI: 앗, 죄송해요!
User: 괜찮아요.
AI: 많이 놀랐죠?

**Outro:**
> She adjusts her bag nervously, still looking at you.
>

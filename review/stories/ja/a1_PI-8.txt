# PI-8: 一緒に旅行しない？


---

### sim1: Where to Go

**Goal:** To suggest a trip, pick a destination together, and start getting excited about the plan.

**📍 장소:** カフェ

**intro_text:**
A long weekend is coming. Your friend has no plans. This is your moment.

**기 — 旅行の提案 [세트 1]**
User: 来週の連休何する？
AI: まだ何もない！なんで？
User: 一緒に旅行しない？
AI: やばい！いいね！どこ？

**Outro:**
> Road trip confirmed! 🗺️ Now the real question: where are we going?
> Kyoto or Osaka? The debate begins.
>

**승 — 決める [세트 2]**
User: 京都どう？ / 大阪行きたい！
AI: いいね！私も行きたかった！
AI: 何日行く？
User: 一泊二日？ / 二泊三日？

**Outro:**
> Destination locked! 📍 Now let's figure out how to get there.
> Transport choices incoming — budget vs. comfort.
>

### sim2: Planning

**Goal:** To decide on transport and accommodation, and split the planning tasks with your friend.

**📍 장소:** カフェ (同じ)

**승 — 計画 [세트 3]**
AI: 何で行く？新幹線？バス？
User: 新幹線にしよう！ / バス安い？
AI: 新幹線が早い！2時間くらいで着くよ！
User: いいね！

**Outro:**
> Shinkansen it is! 🚅 Fast, smooth, and very Japanese.
> Next up: where are we sleeping?
>

**전 — 宿泊 [세트 4]**
AI: 宿どうする？ホテル？ゲストハウス？
User: ゲストハウス！安いから！ / ホテルの方が楽じゃない？
AI: ゲストハウス予約するね！
User: うん、ありがとう！

**Outro:**
> Accommodation sorted! 🏠 Budget travel wins again.
> Now the fun part — what are we actually doing there?
>

### sim3: Getting Excited

**Goal:** To share what you each want to do on the trip and end the planning session buzzing with excitement.

**📍 장소:** カフェ (同じ)

**전 — ワクワク [세트 5]**
User: 京都で何したい？

**Outro:**
> The itinerary is basically writing itself! 🌸 Shrines, matcha, bamboo — perfect.
> The countdown to departure starts now.
>

AI: 楽しみだね！
User: 私も！早く行きたい！
AI: じゃあその時ね！荷物まとめといて！
User: わかった！

**Outro:**
> Bags packed (mentally)! 🧳 This trip is going to be legendary.
> Japan is waiting — and so is the best matcha of your life.

# PI-5: 一緒にご飯食べに行こうよ


---

### sim1: Choosing a Place

**Goal:** To suggest a meal idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**📍 장소:** 渋谷の路上

**intro_text:**
Lunchtime in Tokyo. Endless options. What are we eating?

**기 — 何食べる [세트 1]**
User: お腹すいた！何食べようか？
AI: 私もお腹すいた！何食べたい？
User: 焼肉食べたい！ / 焼肉どう？
AI: いいね！おいしい店知ってる！行こう！

**Outro:**
> Yakiniku it is! 🥩 Now the real question: how far is it?
> Lace up your shoes — it might be a walk.
>

**승 — 行き方 [세트 2]**
AI: ここから近いよ！歩いて行く？
User: うん、歩こう！ / どのくらいかかる？
AI: 10分くらいかな！行こう！
User: わかった！

**Outro:**
> Just a short walk! 🚶 The smell of grilling meat is already calling.
> Almost there — get ready for a feast.
>

---

### sim2: At the Restaurant

**Goal:** To order food together and enjoy yakiniku like a true Tokyo local.

**📍 장소:** 焼肉屋の中

**intro_text:**
We're here. The grill is already hot.

**승 — 食堂 [세트 3]**
AI: ここだよ！どう？
User: わあ、いいね！ / 人多い！
AI: 何頼む？メニュー見て！
User: 牛タン！ / カルビ！

**Outro:**
> Order placed! 🔥 Now comes the most important question in Japan...
> Who manages the grill? The real power move starts now.
>

**전 — 食べる [세트 4]**
AI: 焼いてあげようか？
User: うん、焼いて！ / 自分で焼く！
AI: おいしい！
User: 本当においしい！ / ここ来てよかった！

**Outro:**
> That really hit the spot! 😋 But the real battle is about to begin: paying the bill.
> Brace yourself — the Japanese bill fight is legendary.
>

---

### sim3: After Eating

**Goal:** To insist on paying the bill and make plans to keep the hangout going.

**📍 장소:** 焼肉屋の中 → 外の路上

**전 — 食後 [세트 5]**
User: 私が払う！
AI: いや、割り勘にしよう！
User: いや、今日は私が出す！
AI: 本当に？ありがとう！次は私が払う！

**Outro:**
> You smooth talker, you bought lunch! 😎 Where to next with your full stomach?
> The hangout isn't over yet — round two incoming.
>

**결 — 次は [세트 6]**
AI: お腹いっぱい！次何する？
User: カフェ行く？ / ちょっと歩く？
AI: いいね、行こう！
User: 行こう！

**Outro:**
> Food coma incoming! ☕ Time to grab some coffee and keep the vibe going.
> A perfect hangout deserves a perfect ending.
>

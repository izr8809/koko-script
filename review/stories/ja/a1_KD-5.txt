---

### sim1: Confession to a Friend

**Goal:** To confess to your best friend that you have a crush and share details about who they are.

**📍 장소:** カフェ

**intro_text:**
Two coffees. One secret. Here we go...

**기 — 秘密 [세트 1]**
User: 私ちょっと秘密があるんだけど。
AI: 何？教えて！
User: 最近好きな人いるんだよね。
AI: え、本当に？誰？！

**Outro:**
> The secret is out! 🤫 Now, who is this lucky person?
> Your friend is already losing their mind.
>

**승 — 誰 [세트 2]**
AI: もしかして私が知ってる人？
User: うん。 / ううん、知らないと思う。
AI: どんな人なの？
User: 本当に優しい。 / 笑った時かわいい。

**Outro:**
> A perfect smile? Sounds like a main lead. ✨ Now, how did this romance begin?
> The origin story is always the best part.

---

### sim1: Telling the Story

**Goal:** To tell your friend all about the concert and relive the most exciting moments.

**📍 장소:** カフェ (友達に話す)

**intro_text:**
You just got back from the concert. Your hands are still shaking.

**기 — 行ってきた [세트 1]**
User: 昨日ライブ行ってきた！
AI: (友達) やばい！どうだった？
User: 本当に震えた！ / 最高だった！
AI: うらやましい！何したの？

**Outro:**
> The story begins! 😱 Your friend is already jealous.
> And it gets better — you actually got close to the stage.
>

**승 — 話 [세트 2]**
User: 最前列近くで見れて、目が合った気がした！
AI: え！何それ！すごい！
User: 「大好きです！」って叫んだ！ / 手振ったら振り返してくれた！
AI: 最高じゃん！何て言ってた？

**Outro:**
> You did WHAT?! 🤯 The audacity. The courage. The legend.
> And their reaction? You need to hear this.
>

# KP-5: この歌詞ってどういう意味？


---

### sim1: Asking About Lyrics

**Goal:** To ask your friend about Japanese lyrics you don't understand and learn some key phrases.

**📍 장소:** 友達の家 (一緒に音楽を聴きながら)

**intro_text:**
Their new song is on repeat. But what does it actually mean?

**기 — 歌詞 [세트 1]**
User: この歌何て言ってるの？
AI: (友達) どの部分？
User: 「会いたい」この部分！
AI: 「I miss you」って意味だよ！

**Outro:**
> "会いたい" — now you'll never forget it! 💭 What else don't you know?
> The vocabulary lesson is just getting started.
>

**승 — もっと知る [세트 2]**
User: 「好きだよ」は知ってる！じゃあ「愛してる」は？
AI: それは「I love you」！「好きだよ」より強い気持ち！
User: あ、違うんだ！ / 本当に？
AI: うん！日本語の歌詞って面白いでしょ？

**Outro:**
> Love vs. like — the eternal J-Pop distinction! 🎵 Now you know the difference.
> Time to put that knowledge to use and actually sing along.
>

---

### sim2: Singing Along

**Goal:** To try singing along to a Japanese song and get encouragement from your friend.

**📍 장소:** 友達の家 (同じ)

**승 — 一緒に歌う [세트 3]**
AI: この歌一緒に歌ってみる？
User: いいよ！ / 歌詞知らなくても大丈夫？
AI: 大丈夫！真似してみて！
User: わかった！

**Outro:**
> Karaoke mode activated! 🎤 Don't worry about the pronunciation — just feel it.
> Your friend is about to give you the best compliment.
>

**전 — 歌う [세트 4]**
AI: 上手じゃん！
User: 本当に？ / 発音変じゃない？
AI: 全然！上手だよ！センスある！
User: ありがとう！

**Outro:**
> "センスある!" — you've got the feel! 🌟 That's the highest compliment.
> Now let's talk about the new song everyone's obsessing over.
>

---

### sim3: New Song

**Goal:** To bond over a new release, discuss the emotional lyrics, and plan to listen together.

**📍 장소:** 友達の家 (同じ)

**전 — 新しい曲 [세트 5]**
User: 最近新しい曲出たじゃん？聴いた？
AI: うん！すごくいい！君は？
User: 私も聴いた！歌詞が切ないね。
AI: そう！「待ってるよ」の部分本当に切ない。

**Outro:**
> "待ってるよ" — I'll wait for you. 😢 Why is J-Pop always so emotional?
> End the session the only way possible: listening together.
>

**결 — 一緒に聴く [세트 6]**
User: 一緒に聴きながら歌詞教えて！
AI: いいよ！聴いてみて！
User: この曲本当にいい。
AI: そう！

**Outro:**
> Music + friendship + Japanese lyrics = perfect afternoon! 🎶 You're basically fluent now.
> Next up: a fan story that will make you want to go to a live immediately.
>

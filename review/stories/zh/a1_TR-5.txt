# TR-5: 我想办理入住手续


---

### sim1: 办理入住

**Goal:** To check into your hotel, confirm your booking details, and get your room key.

**📍 장소:** 酒店前台

**intro_text:**
Finally at the hotel. Time to check in.

**기 — 到达 [세트 1]**
User: 您好，我要入住。
AI: 您好！请问您预订了吗？
User: 是的，我叫___。
AI: 好的，查到了！请出示一下您的身份证。

**Outro:**
> ID checked! 🛂 Let's see if they got your booking right.
> One more step before you get that room key.
>

**승 — 确认房间 [세트 2]**
AI: 您是住两晚吗？
User: 是的，没错。/ 不是，住三晚。
AI: 您的房间在8楼，这是您的房卡！
User: 谢谢您！

**Outro:**
> Room key secured! 🔑 Now, onto the most important question: the WiFi.
> A hotel room without WiFi is just a very expensive nap.
>

### sim2: 问问题

**Goal:** To ask the front desk about WiFi, breakfast hours, and request extra towels for your room.

**📍 장소:** 酒店前台（同一地点）

**승 — 询问 [세트 3]**
User: 请问WiFi密码是多少？
AI: 写在这张卡上！
User: 谢谢！请问早餐几点开始？
AI: 早上7点到9点！在一楼餐厅！

**Outro:**
> Breakfast schedule locked in! 🍳 But wait, do you need anything extra for the room?
> Never leave the front desk without asking for towels.
>

**전 — 额外需求 [세트 4]**
User: 请问可以多给我几条毛巾吗？
AI: 好的，我让人送到您房间！
User: 谢谢您！
AI: 还有什么需要的吗？

**Outro:**
> Extra towels on the way! 🛁 Time to hunt for some late-night snacks.
> A convenience store run is basically a China travel ritual.
>

### sim3: 安顿下来

**Goal:** To find out where the nearest convenience store is and wrap up your check-in smoothly.

**📍 장소:** 酒店大堂

**전 — 安顿 [세트 5]**
User: 请问附近有便利店吗？
AI: 出门右转就有一家全家！
User: 谢谢您！
AI: 请慢慢休息！

**Outro:**
> Convenience store located! 🏪 You're officially ready to settle in.
> Instant noodles, snacks, and a good night's sleep — the perfect combo.
>

**결 — 结束 [세트 6]**
AI: 有什么需要请打电话给前台！
User: 好的，谢谢！
AI: 祝您住得愉快！
User: 谢谢！

**Outro:**
> Check-in complete! Rest up. 🛏️ Next up: Surviving the Beijing subway maze.
> Tomorrow's adventure starts with a transit card.
>

# PI-5: 咱们去吃饭吧！


---

### sim1: 选地方

**Goal:** To suggest a meal idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**📍 장소:** 三里屯街头

**intro_text:**
Lunchtime in Beijing. Endless options. What are we eating?

**기 — 吃什么 [세트 1]**
User: 我饿了！吃什么好？
AI: 我也饿了！你想吃什么？
User: 想吃烤肉！/ 吃烤肉怎么样？
AI: 好啊！我知道一家超好吃的！走吧！

**Outro:**
> Kǎoròu it is! 🥩 Now, how far is this place?
> Your stomach is already counting down.
>

**승 — 怎么去 [세트 2]**
AI: 就在附近！走路去吧！
User: 好！要走多久？/ 走路去啊？
AI: 五分钟就到了！走吧！
User: 好的！

**Outro:**
> Five minutes away! 🚶 The smell of grilling meat is basically pulling you there.
> Almost there — let's go!
>

### sim2: 在餐厅

**Goal:** To order food together and enjoy the meal like a true Beijing local.

**📍 장소:** 烤肉店里

**intro_text:**
You're here. The place looks packed. That's always a good sign.

**승 — 点菜 [세트 3]**
AI: 到了！怎么样？
User: 哇，不错！/ 人好多！
AI: 点什么？这是菜单！
User: 烤肉两人份！/ 再来个凉菜！

**Outro:**
> Order placed! 🥩 Now comes the most important question in China...
> Who's in charge of the grill? The real power move starts now.
>

**전 — 吃饭 [세트 4]**
AI: 我来烤吧！
User: 好，你来！/ 我来烤！
AI: 超好吃！
User: 真的超好吃！/ 这家找对了！

**Outro:**
> That really hit the spot! 😋 But the real battle is about to begin: paying the bill.
> Brace yourself — the Chinese bill fight is legendary.
>

### sim3: 吃完了

**Goal:** To insist on paying the bill and make plans to keep the hangout going.

**📍 장소:** 烤肉店里 → 店外街头

**전 — 结账 [세트 5]**
User: 我来付！
AI: 不行，一起付！
User: 不用，今天我请！
AI: 真的？谢谢！下次我请！

**Outro:**
> You smooth talker, you bought lunch! 😎 Where to next with your full stomach?
> The hangout isn't over yet — round two incoming.
>

**결 — 下一站 [세트 6]**
AI: 吃饱了！现在去哪儿？
User: 去喝奶茶？/ 走走消化一下？
AI: 好，走吧！
User: 走！

**Outro:**
> Food coma incoming! 🧋 Time to grab some milk tea and keep the vibe going.
> A perfect hangout deserves a perfect ending.
>

# CR-5: 您吃午饭了吗？


---

### sim1: 午饭时间

**Goal:** To accept a lunch invitation from a coworker and pick a restaurant together.

**📍 장소:** 办公室

**intro_text:**
12:30 PM. Your stomach is growling. A coworker appears.

**기 — 午饭 [세트 1]**
AI: 您吃午饭了吗？
User: 还没有。/ 是的，吃了。
AI: 一起去吃吧？
User: 好的，谢谢！/ 好，太好了！

**Outro:**
> Lunch invitation accepted! 🍱 Now the important question: where are we going?
> Your coworker has a recommendation — and it sounds amazing.
>

**승 — 菜单 [세트 2]**
AI: 吃什么好？附近有好吃的！
User: 什么都可以！/ 有什么好吃的？
AI: 这里的麻辣烫很有名！
User: 好！走吧！

**Outro:**
> Malatang it is! 🌶️ The ultimate Chinese office lunch.
> Time to bond over a bowl of something spicy.
>

### sim2: 吃饭中

**Goal:** To navigate the menu, handle the spice question, and have a real conversation with your coworker.

**📍 장소:** 公司附近餐厅

**승 — 吃饭中 [세트 3]**
AI: 中国菜吃得惯吗？能吃辣吗？
User: 可以！没问题！/ 有点辣的有点难。
AI: 那点个不辣的？清汤锅底怎么样？
User: 好的！

**Outro:**
> Clear broth: the safe and delicious choice! 🥣 Your coworker is looking out for you.
> Now the real workplace bonding begins.
>

**전 — 聊天 [세트 4]**
AI: 在中国生活怎么样？适应了吗？
User: 慢慢适应了。/ 有时候有点难。
AI: 有什么难的就说！我帮你！

**Outro:**
> A coworker who actually cares! ☕ That's rare and precious.
> Now comes the awkward part: who pays?

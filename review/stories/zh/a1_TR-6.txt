# TR-6: 请问怎么坐地铁？


---

### sim1: 在地铁站

**Goal:** To buy a transit card, charge it, and find the right subway line to your destination.

**📍 장소:** 地铁站售票机前

**intro_text:**
Beijing subway. 22 lines. Hundreds of stations. You've got this.

**기 — 在站里 [세트 1]**
User: 请问，交通卡在哪里买？
AI: 在那边的机器上可以买！
User: 谢谢！充值也在那里吗？
AI: 是的，可以的！

**Outro:**
> Transit card acquired! 💳 Now, which line do you need?
> Beijing's subway map looks scary — but it's actually pretty logical.
>

**승 — 找线路 [세트 2]**
User: 我想去三里屯，坐几号线？
AI: 坐10号线！在那边！
User: 需要换乘吗？
AI: 不用，直达！

**Outro:**
> Line 10, no transfers! 🚇 That's the best news you've heard all day.
> Now hop on and try not to miss your stop.
>

---

### sim2: 在地铁上

**Goal:** To ask a fellow passenger for help and figure out how many stops to your destination.

**📍 장소:** 地铁车厢里

**승 — 在车上 [세트 3]**
AI: （旁边的人）您好，请问您去哪儿？
User: 去三里屯！
AI: 那您再坐三站就到了！
User: 三站？谢谢您！

**Outro:**
> Three more stops! 🎯 A kind stranger just saved your day.
> But wait — what if you need to transfer somewhere else?
>

**전 — 换乘 [세트 4]**
User: 请问，在国贸站怎么换乘？
AI: 下车之后看指示牌，跟着1号线走就行！
User: 谢谢您！
AI: 很简单的！别担心！

**Outro:**
> Transfer decoded! 🗺️ Follow the signs and you'll be fine.
> Almost at your destination — just a few more stops.
>

---

### sim3: 到站了

**Goal:** To confirm you've arrived at the right station and find the correct exit.

**📍 장소:** 地铁站（到达站台）

**전 — 到达 [세트 5]**
User: 这里是三里屯站吗？
AI: 是的，没错！
User: 请问从几号出口出去？
AI: 从C出口出去！

**Outro:**
> Exit C! 🚪 You made it through the Beijing subway like a pro.
> One last thank-you and you're free.
>

**결 — 出站 [세트 6]**
User: 谢谢您！
AI: 不客气！
User: 真的谢谢您！
AI: 祝您玩得开心！

**Outro:**
> Beijing subway: conquered! 🏆 You're officially a local now.
> Next challenge: shopping in Sanlitun without spending your whole budget.
>

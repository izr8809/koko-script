# KD-7: 我们在一起吧？


---

### sim1: 这一刻

**Goal:** To finally confess your feelings and hear the answer you've been hoping for.

**📍 장소:** 外滩（晚上）

**intro_text:**
The Bund at night. You said you had something to say. This is it.

**기 — 表白 [세트 1]**
AI: 你说今天有话要说。是什么？
User: 有点紧张。/ 你靠近一点。
AI: 为什么紧张？说吧！
User: 我……喜欢你。

**Outro:**
> You said it! 💘 Three words that change everything.
> Now hold your breath and wait for the answer.
>

**승 — 回答 [세트 2]**
AI: 真的？我也喜欢你。
User: 真的？/ 从什么时候？
AI: 从第一次见面就开始了。
User: 我也是。

**Outro:**
> Mutual feelings confirmed! 🌅 The Bund just witnessed a drama moment.
> So... does this mean what we think it means?
>

### sim2: 正式在一起

**Goal:** To officially become a couple and start planning your first date together.

**📍 장소:** 外滩（同一地点）

**승 — 在一起 [세트 3]**
AI: 那我们在一起吧？
User: 好。/ 对吧？
AI: 太好了。
User: 我也很开心。

**Outro:**
> Official! 💑 You are now a couple. Congratulations.
> First order of business: plan the perfect first date.
>

**전 — 第一次约会 [세트 4]**
AI: 在一起之后第一次约会！想去哪儿？
User: 想去看海！/ 去吃好吃的吧！
AI: 好！周末去！
User: 好期待！

**Outro:**
> First date planned! 🗓️ The excitement is real.
> But first — the sweetest part of any new relationship.
>

### sim3: 甜蜜

**Goal:** To exchange sweet messages after the big night and end with a tender goodnight.

**📍 장소:** 各自家里（微信聊天）

**전 — 心动 [세트 5]**
AI: 到家了吗？
User: 嗯，刚到！
AI: 今天真的很开心。
User: 我也是。/ 一直在想你。

**Outro:**
> Late night texts after a perfect evening! 📱 This feeling is everything.
> One last message before you both fall asleep smiling.
>

**결 — 晚安 [세트 6]**
AI: 明天还想见你。
User: 我也想见你。
AI: 晚安。做个好梦。
User: 晚安。

**Outro:**
> "做个好梦。" 😴 The most romantic goodnight in Chinese.
> Sweet dreams — you're going to need the energy for what comes next.
>

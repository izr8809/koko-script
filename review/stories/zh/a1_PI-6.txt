# PI-6: 我学中文是因为你


---

### sim1: 说真心话

**Goal:** To open up about why you're learning Chinese and feel supported by your friend's encouragement.

**📍 장소:** 咖啡馆

**intro_text:**
A quiet café. A real conversation. This one goes deeper.

**기 — 深聊 [세트 1]**
AI: 你为什么学中文？
User: 想交中国朋友！/ 想看中国剧！
AI: 哦，真的？你现在学得挺好的！
User: 还不行呢。/ 真的？谢谢！

**Outro:**
> You're doing better than you think! 🌟 But how's life in China, really?
> Time to get honest about the hard parts.
>

**승 — 在中国的生活 [세트 2]**
AI: 在中国生活有没有觉得难的地方？
User: 有时候很难受。/ 中文还不好。
AI: 难受的时候跟我说！
User: 谢谢。/ 好，我会的。

**Outro:**
> You've got a real friend right here. 🤝 That means everything.
> And this friendship is only getting stronger.
>

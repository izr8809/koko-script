# KD-6: 你误会我了


---

### sim1: 误会了

**Goal:** To face a jealous accusation, stay calm, and explain the situation clearly.

**📍 장소:** 公园长椅

**intro_text:**
A park bench. A tense silence. Someone saw something they didn't understand.

**기 — 误会 [세트 1]**
AI: 你昨天为什么跟别人在一起？
User: 什么？你说什么？/ 你误会我了！
AI: 我看到了。在咖啡馆。
User: 那个人只是朋友！/ 啊，那个时候？

**Outro:**
> Caught! 😬 But it's not what it looks like — time to explain.
> The truth is simple, but jealousy makes everything complicated.
>

**승 — 解释 [세트 2]**
AI: 真的只是朋友？
User: 是，真的。是同学。/ 相信我！
AI: 对不起。我有点吃醋了。
User: 没关系。/ 我懂了。

**Outro:**
> Jealousy admitted! 💔 That took courage. Now let's actually talk about it.
> The real conversation is just beginning.
>

---

### sim2: 好好说

**Goal:** To address the misunderstanding directly and agree to communicate better next time.

**📍 장소:** 公园长椅（同一地点）

**승 — 沟通 [세트 3]**
User: 为什么不问我？
AI: 不太好意思问。
User: 下次直接问我。别误会。
AI: 好，对不起。

**Outro:**
> Communication rules established! 📋 This is how healthy relationships work.
> Now it's time to actually forgive and move on.
>

**전 — 和好 [세트 4]**
AI: 我真的很抱歉。
User: 没事。但是要相信我。
AI: 嗯，相信你。以后不会这样了。
User: 好。

**Outro:**
> Trust restored! 🤝 That's what matters most.
> Now let's get out of this park and do something fun.
>

---

### sim3: 和好了

**Goal:** To fully make up, agree to grab food together, and end on a sweet note.

**📍 장소:** 公园 → 街上（走着）

**전 — 和解 [세트 5]**
AI: 气消了吗？
User: 消了。/ 差不多了。
AI: 去吃点好吃的吧。我请。
User: 好啊！

**Outro:**
> Food fixes everything! 🍽️ The classic Chinese makeup move.
> One last sweet moment before the credits roll.
>

**결 — 重新开始 [세트 6]**
AI: 下次直接问！
User: 好！
AI: 喜欢你。
User: 我也是。

**Outro:**
> "我也是。" Two words. Maximum impact. 💕 Drama ending unlocked.
> This couple just got stronger — misunderstanding and all.
>

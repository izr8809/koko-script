# TR-8: 这里有什么著名的？


---

### sim1: 问当地人

**Goal:** To ask a local for food recommendations and find out about a famous spot nearby.

**📍 장소:** 老街小巷

**intro_text:**
You're in a new neighborhood. No plan, no map. Just vibes and curiosity.

**기 — 推荐 [세트 1]**
User: 请问，这附近有什么好吃的吗？
AI: 有！那边有一家很有名的麻辣烫！
User: 哦，想去试试！远吗？
AI: 不远，走路五分钟就到了！

**Outro:**
> Local tip unlocked! 🌶️ Five minutes away — that's basically next door.
> But wait, there's more to this neighborhood than just food.
>

**승 — 多问一点 [세트 2]**
User: 这里有什么著名的？
AI: 这条街的老建筑很有名！有没有去过老街？
User: 还没有！
AI: 一定要去！拍照很好看！

**Outro:**
> Old street added to the list! 📸 This neighborhood just got a lot more interesting.
> Time to step inside and see what the hype is about.
>

### sim2: 参观

**Goal:** To visit the old street, admire the space, and order something famous from a local stall.

**📍 장소:** 老街里

**승 — 参观 [세트 3]**
User: 这里就是那条老街吗？
AI: 是的！进来看看吧！
User: 哇，真的好漂亮！
AI: 谢谢！您想吃点什么吗？

**Outro:**
> Traditional architecture meets street food culture! 🏯 Stunning.
> Now, what's the must-try item here?
>

**전 — 点单 [세트 4]**
User: 这里什么最好吃？
AI: 我们的糖葫芦很有名！
User: 来一串糖葫芦！
AI: 好的，稍等一下！

**Outro:**
> Tanghulu incoming! 🍡 The most iconic Chinese street snack.
> This is going to be worth every yuan.
>

**Goal:** To enjoy your snack, compliment the street, and promise to come back.

**📍 장소:** 老街里（同一地点）

**Outro:**
> Tanghulu approved! 😍 This place is going straight into your travel diary.

AI: 谢谢您！欢迎再来！

> A perfect afternoon on a perfect street! ✨ China keeps delivering.

# KP-5: 这句歌词是什么意思？


---

### sim1: 问歌词

**Goal:** To ask your friend about lyrics you don't understand and learn some key phrases.

**📍 장소:** 朋友家（一起听音乐）

**intro_text:**
Jay Chou's new song is on repeat. But what does it actually mean?

**기 — 歌词 [세트 1]**
User: 这首歌在唱什么？
AI: （朋友）哪一句？
User: "等你回来"这一句！
AI: 就是"waiting for you to come back"的意思！

**Outro:**
> "等你回来" — now you'll never forget it! 💭 What else don't you know?
> The vocabulary lesson is just getting started.
>

**승 — 多学一点 [세트 2]**
User: "想你"我知道！那"思念"呢？
AI: 那是"miss you"！比"想你"更文艺！
User: 哦，不一样！/ 真的？
AI: 对！中文歌词很有意思吧？

**Outro:**
> Miss vs. miss — the eternal C-pop distinction! 🎵 Now you know the difference.
> Time to put that knowledge to use and actually sing along.
>

**📍 장소:** 朋友家（同一地点）

**Outro:**
> Karaoke mode activated! 🎤 Don't worry about the pronunciation — just feel it.

**Outro:**
> "有感觉！" — you've got the feel! 🌟 That's the highest compliment.
> Now let's talk about the new song everyone's obsessing over.

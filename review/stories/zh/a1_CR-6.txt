# CR-6: 我明天可以请假吗？


---

### sim1: 申请请假

**Goal:** To politely request a day off from your manager and explain the reason clearly.

**📍 장소:** 办公室（经理位置前）

**intro_text:**
You need tomorrow off. Your manager is right there. Deep breath.

**기 — 申请 [세트 1]**
User: 经理，我有事。
AI: 好的，请说。
User: 明天我可以请假吗？
AI: 明天？有急事吗？

**Outro:**
> The ask is out there! 😬 Now explain yourself calmly and professionally.
> This is the moment that separates the rookies from the pros.
>

**승 — 解释 [세트 2]**
User: 是的，需要去医院。/ 有点私事。
AI: 好的，明白了。
AI: 明天的工作可以交给别人吗？
User: 可以，我来整理。

**Outro:**
> Proactive handover offer! 📋 That's exactly what a good employee does.
> Now wait for the official answer.
>

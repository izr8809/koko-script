### sim1: Algo está mal

**Goal:** To open up about what's been bothering you and let your friend in on your struggles.

**📍 장소:** Cafetería

**intro_text:**
Something's been off lately. Your friend noticed. Time to talk.

**기 — El problema [세트 1]**
AI: Últimamente te noto raro/a. ¿Estás bien?
User: Estoy un poco mal. / Tengo algo en mente.
AI: ¿Qué tienes? Cuéntame.
User: La vida aquí es difícil. / Tengo mucho trabajo.

**Outro:**
> It's out there now. 💬 The first step is always the hardest.
> Your friend is listening — keep going.
>

**승 — Escuchando [세트 2]**

> Now it's time to do something about it.

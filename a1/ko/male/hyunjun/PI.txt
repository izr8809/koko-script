# PI-5: 우리 밥 먹으러 가자

- 대분류: Personal Interest
- 중분류: Hanging Out
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: Choosing a Place

**Goal:** To suggest a food idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**📍 장소:** 길거리

**intro_text:**
Lunch time in Korea. So many options... what should we eat?

**기 — 뭐 먹을까 [세트 1]**
User: 배고파! 뭐 먹을까?
AI: 나도 배고파! 뭐 먹고 싶어?
User: 삼겹살 먹고 싶어! / 삼겹살 어때?
AI: 좋아! 맛있는 데 알아! 가자!

**Outro:**
> Samgyeopsal it is! 🥓 But wait, how do we get there?
> Prepare your stomach — a massive feast awaits.
>

**승 — 가는 길 [세트 2]**
AI: 여기서 가까워! 걸어갈까?
User: 응, 걸어가자! / 얼마나 걸리는데?
AI: 5분이면 돼! 가자!
User: 알겠어!

**Outro:**
> Just a short walk! 🚶‍♂️ Prepare your stomach for a massive feast.
> Almost there... the smell of grilling meat is calling.
>

---

### sim2: At the Restaurant

**Goal:** To order food together and bond over grilling meat like a true Korean local.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안

**intro_text:**
We're here. This place looks amazing.

**승 — 식당 [세트 3]**
AI: 여기야! 어때?
User: 와, 좋다! / 사람 많다!
AI: 뭐 시킬까? 여기 메뉴판!
User: 삼겹살 2인분 / 된장찌개!

**Outro:**
> Order placed! 🥩 Now comes the most important question in Korea...
> Who grills the meat? The real power move starts now.
>

**전 — 먹기 [세트 4]**
AI: 고기 구워줄까?
User: 응, 구워줘! / 내가 구울게!
AI: 맛있다!
User: 진짜 맛있다! / 여기 잘 찾았다!

**Outro:**
> That really hit the spot! 😋 But the real battle is about to begin: paying the bill.
> Brace yourself — the Korean bill fight is legendary.
>

---

### sim3: After Eating

**Goal:** To insist on paying the bill and make plans to keep the hangout going.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안 → 식당 밖 길거리

**전 — 식사 후 [세트 5]**

AI: 아니야, 같이 내자!
User: 아니야, 오늘 내가 쏠게!
AI: 진짜? 고마워! 다음엔 내가 살게!

**Outro:**
> You smooth talker, you bought lunch! 😎 Where to next with your full stomach?
> The hangout isn't over yet — round two incoming.
>

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**
AI: 배불러! 이제 뭐 할까?
User: 카페 갈까? / 좀 걸을까?
AI: 좋아, 가자!
User: 가자!

**Outro:**
> Food coma incoming! ☕ Time to grab some coffee and keep the vibe going.
> A perfect hangout deserves a perfect ending.

# PI-7: 고민 있어?

- 중분류: Deep Talk
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: Something's Wrong

**Goal:** To open up about what's been bothering you and let your friend in on your struggles.

**📍 장소:** 카페

**intro_text:**
Something's been off lately. Your friend noticed. Time to talk.

**기 — 고민 [세트 1]**

User: 좀 힘들어. / 고민이 있어.

**Outro:**
> It's out there now. 💬 The first step is always the hardest.
> Your friend is listening — keep going.
>

**승 — 들어주기 [세트 2]**
AI: 어떤 게 제일 힘들어?
User: 혼자 있는 시간이 많아서. / 한국어가 아직 어려워서.

User: 응, 좀.

**Outro:**
> Heard and understood. 🫂 That's what real friends do.
> Now it's time to do something about it.

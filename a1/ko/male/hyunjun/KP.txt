# KP-1: 왔어요?

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Fan Meeting
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: First Sign

**Goal:** To stand in front of Hyunjun at the fan sign and actually hold a conversation.

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 사인회 (테이블)

**intro_text:**
He's already seated.
Pen in hand. Eyes down.
You step forward. He looks up.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
User: 안녕하세요! 만나서 정말 반가워요!
AI: …네, 앉아요. 기다렸어요?
User: 네, 오래 기다렸어요. 드디어 왔어요!
AI: 수고했어요. 멀리서 왔어요?

**Outro:**
> He asked where you came from. He was paying attention. 🪑
> He's about to write your name.
>

**승 — 사인 [세트 2]**
User: 사인 해주세요! 이름도 써줄 수 있어요?
AI: 이름 말해요.
User: ___이에요! 잘 부탁드려요!
AI: …됐어요. 천천히 썼어요.

**Outro:**
> He wrote it slowly. He didn't rush. ✍️
> He noticed something. Ask before the moment passes.
>

- 대분류: Traveling
- 중분류: Bakery

- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 주문하기

**Goal:** To order bread and a drink at Taeo's favorite Yeonnam-dong bakery.

**📍 장소:** 연남동 빵집 카운터

The smell of fresh bread hits you at the door.

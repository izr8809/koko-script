### sim1: 처음 만남

**Goal:** To meet Taeo at the fan sign and actually hold a conversation with him.

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 사인회 (테이블)

**intro_text:**
He's already looking at you.
Not past you. At you.
Like he's been waiting for this exact moment.

User: 네! 정말 기다렸어요!

User: 맞아요, 날씨 진짜 좋더라고요!

**Outro:**
> He noticed the sky before he noticed the crowd. 🌤️
> Now he's noticing you.
>

**Outro:**

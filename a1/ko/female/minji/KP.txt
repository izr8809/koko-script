# KP-4: 언니 생일 축하해요!

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Special Event
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: Birthday Message

**Goal:** To send your idol a heartfelt birthday message and tease her about a surprise gift you sent.

**📍 장소:** 온라인 (버블/위버스 메시지)

**intro_text:**
Midnight KST. It's officially her birthday. My heart is racing...

**기 — 생일 [세트 1]**
User: 생일 축하해요!
AI: 감사해요! 고마워요!
User: 오늘 뭐 했어요?
AI: 멤버들이랑 케이크 먹었어요!

**Outro:**
> First birthday message delivered! 🎂 Did she get your special gift yet?
> The suspense is killing you — and her.
>

**승 — 선물 [세트 2]**
User: 선물 보냈어요! 받았어요?
AI: 아직 못 받았어요! 뭔데요?
User: 비밀이에요! / 편지랑 선물이요!
AI: 기대할게요! 감사해요!

**Outro:**
> Gift successfully sent! 🎁 Alert: She just started a surprise live stream!
> Drop everything — the birthday live is starting.
>

# KP-5: 가사가 무슨 뜻이에요?

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Understanding Lyrics
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: Asking About Lyrics

**Goal:** To ask your friend about Korean lyrics you don't understand and learn some key phrases.

**📍 장소:** 친구 집 (같이 음악 들으면서)

**intro_text:**
Her new song is on repeat. But what does it actually mean?

**기 — 가사 [세트 1]**
User: 이 노래 뭐라고 하는 거야?
AI: 어떤 부분?
User: "보고 싶다" 이 부분!
AI: "I miss you"라는 뜻이야!

**Outro:**
> "보고 싶다" — now you'll never forget it! 💭 What else don't you know?
> The vocabulary lesson is just getting started.
>

**승 — 더 알기 [세트 2]**
User: "사랑해"는 알아! 그러면 "좋아해"는?
AI: 그건 "I like you"! "사랑해"보다 가벼워!
User: 아, 다르구나! / 진짜?
AI: 응! 한국어 가사 재밌지?

**Outro:**
> Love vs. like — the eternal K-pop distinction! 🎵 Now you know the difference.
> Time to put that knowledge to use and actually sing along.
>

**Outro:**

> Your friend is about to give you the best compliment.

**전 — 부르기 [세트 4]**

**Outro:**

> Now let's talk about the new song everyone's obsessing over.
>

**Goal:** To tell your friend all about the fan meeting and relive the most exciting moments.

**📍 장소:** 카페 (친구한테 얘기)

**intro_text:**
